So what is it?

ClubLink allows instant communication and updates between Golf Club officials/team managers, and club members. We provide a free iPhone and Android app, and once set up, clubs can post news, results and fixtures to the app. Each club can configure the app to display different results for any of its Lady, Male or Junior members.

The user can also find current weather conditions in the area, along with all details and notifications posted by the club. Updating info to the app is easy - ClubLink makes use of the super-simple Google Docs, and any updates are sent automatically and instantly to all users of the app.

Getting your club set up to use ClubLink is simple - head to the pricing page to see how you feel about our costs, and so we can show you just how easy setup and updating is.
