Posting club news to ClubLink - the 10-step solution

There are just a few simple steps required to start using ClubLink, and once set up, it's free forever!


1. Have a look at our pricing, and then email us to provide payment details

2. Create a Google Account for your golf club here

3. Once your Google Account is set up, and you are logged in, sign up for Google Drive here

4. Now, in Google Drive, create a new document, and call it "News". If you need assistance, either look at Google's help documents or email us

5. In this document, type out all the news you want to appear in the app (don't worry, it can be changed and updated whenever you want). This document will be saved automatically

6. Now it is time to publish the document. In the top left hand side of the screen, just under the title of the document, click "file". A drop-down menu should appear. Near the bottom of this menu, click "Publish to the web...". A small box should appear in the center of the screen. Make sure the box beside "Automatically republish when changes are made" is ticked, and then click the "Start Publishing" button. A small popup box will be displayed, asking if you are sure you want to publish the document. Click "OK"

7. After you click "OK", save the text inside the box under "Document Link". You can save it into an email or copy it to your clipboard, or even write it down. This text will look something like this: https://docs.google.com/document/pub?id=1fX_mmaUa2G_OF4mIQP5LOffXvN2Fb4byIjtuWdEvxV0

8. Go back to the Google Drive home page

9. Create two new spreadsheets, one called "Fixtures", and one called "Results". In these spreadsheets, you can type your club's fixtures and results, respectively. Repeat step 6 and 7 for both of these spreadsheets

10. Now it is time to send the document details to ClubLink administrators, so they can be added to the app. To do this, email contact@frostplant.com. In the email, you should include the following: 1: Your Golf Club's name; 2: The first 4 digits of your club's GUI and ILGU numbers (e.g. 3097xxxx); 3: The three URL's you saved from step 7. You will then receive a confirmation email from us, just to let you know that you have done everything all right



It should be noted that if you wish to purchase support for more than one membership type (e.g. Lady and Male members), step 4 through 9 will needed to be repeated for each additional membership type.
