The Basics

With ClubLink, your Golf Club can easily and instantly share news, results and fixtures with all of its members, through ClubLink's iPhone or Android app. Once your club is registered with the GUI (or ILGU) it are eligible to make use of ClubLink. Your club can also choose to set up separate ClubLink pages for any of the membership types it offers, e.g. Lady, Male, and Junior.

Our service can benefit your club in many ways. If your club currently avails of some sort of text service, ClubLink's small, once-off setup fee can greatly reduce your club's costs. It may also reduce the need for a costly website for your club. The most appealing part of ClubLink is its ease of use. Through Google Docs, any appointed member of the club can update the News, Fixtures or results page for their club. It is incredibly simple, and updates instantly


Services

The ClubLink app provides separate tabs for News, Fixtures, Results and Weather. This means that all information relevant to your club's members is instantly available to them at their fingertips. The News, Fixtures and Results tabs are updated by an elected club member/official. Once registered for ClubLink, all necessary information will be sent to this person. It is then maintained through Google Docs, a free, web-based intuitive text editor. Once updates are made to your News, Fixtures or Results documents, they are automatically and instantly shared to all members of your club who have the iPhone or Android app.

We provide our weather information courtesy of Accuweather.com, a free and accurate weather service. This can also prove helpful for club members seeking to plan their tee times.
